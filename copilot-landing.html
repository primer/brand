FAQ

Questions, answered.

What editors and IDEs does Copilot support?

VS Code, Visual Studio, JetBrains IDEs, Neovim, Xcode, and Eclipse. Plus first-class support in the GitHub CLI and on github.com.

How does Copilot use my code?

Your prompts and suggestions are not used to train models for paid plans. Enterprise customers get additional data residency and IP indemnity protections.

Can I choose which model Copilot uses?

Yes. On Pro and Enterprise you can pick between Claude, GPT, and Gemini models from the model picker — including for chat, edits, and the agent.

Does Copilot work offline?

Copilot requires a network connection to call the model providers. Lightweight inline ghost-text uses an edge-cached path for low latency.

Is there a free tier?

Yes. The Free plan gives you 2,000 completions and 50 chats per month — no credit card required. Upgrade to Pro any time.
