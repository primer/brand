Added new `Tabs` component.

Example:

```jsx
<Tabs {...args} aria-label="Tabs">
  <Tabs.Item>Tab one</Tabs.Item>
  <Tabs.Item>Tab two</Tabs.Item>

  <Tabs.Panel>
    <Text>Panel one</Text>
  </Tabs.Panel>
  <Tabs.Panel>
    <Text>Panel two</Text>
  </Tabs.Panel>
</Tabs>
```
